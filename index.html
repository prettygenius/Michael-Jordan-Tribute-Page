How Jordan became the G.O.A.T

  • Michael Jeffrey Jordan born February 17, 1963
  • In college Jordan played for the North Carolina Tar Heels and makes a game-winning jump shot to lead them to the 1982 NCAA Championship against Georgetown
  • Jordan then joins the Chicago Bulls in 1984 as the third overall pick in the NBA draft
  • Jordan would later that season become Rookie of the Year
  • During 90-91 season Jordan wins his second MVP
  • Jordan would also lead the Bulls to first place in their division and set a franchise record with 61 wins in the regular season
  • June 12 1991 Jordan leads the Bulls to win first NBA title in franchise history also wins first finals mvp award
  • In the first 3peat Jordan would lead the Bulls to two more title's in 92 and 93
  • Jordan would then retire later that year to play minor league baseball
  • Although Jordan had not played in the NBA since retirement in 93 on March 28 1995 Jordan scored 55 points against the Knicks in Madison Square Garden also known as the "double nickel"
  • The Bulls were emliminated from the playoffs in the conference semifinals Jordan was determinded to pick up where he left off
  • 1995-1996 season Jordan leads the Bulls to what was then the best NBA regular season record in history
  • Jordan with an average of 30 points per game would go on to win the MVP award for the season as well as the all-star MVP award
  • After only losing a total of 4 games during the playoffs series Jordan led the Bulls to win their 4th title
  • Jordan also wins 4th finals mvp award passing Magic Johnson's three
  • In the 96-97 season Jordan leads the bulls to another great season winning 69-13 and they advance to the finals to play the Utah Jazz
  • During the series Jordan being competitve by nature Jordan played game 5 with "flu like" symptoms and score 38 points against the Jazz
  • Jordan received the finals MVP for the 5th time as the Bulls win the series in 6 games as Well as their 5th title
  • In the 97-98 season Jordan takes the bulls to their second 3peat as they advance to the finals to take on the Utah Jazz in a rematch and named the NBA mvp for the 5th time
  • Also that season Jordan makes the All NBA First team and First Defensive Team and the All Star Game Mvp
  • After defeating the Jazz in game 6 in 98 the Bulls win their sixth title and Jordan win Finals Mvp again
  • Jordan retires for the second time in 1999
  • In 2001 Jordan returns to the NBA to play for the Washington Wizards
  • Jordan passes Kareem Abdul-Jabbar as the all-time leading scorer in All-Star Game history in 2003
  • Also that same year Jordan became the first 40-year-old to score 43 points in an NBA game
  • Among other accolades Jordan also recieved 2 gold medals playing in the Olympics
  • In 2010 Jordan becomes owner of the Bobcats making him the first former player to be majority owner of an NBA team
  • In his career Jordan scored 32,292 regular season points and is now 5th on the all time scroing list
  • 2009 Jordan is inducted into the NBA hall of fame
